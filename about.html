What is Nebula Focus?

Nebula Focus is a modern, beautiful study timer app designed for students who want to maximize their productivity. Built on the proven Pomodoro technique, it combines stunning space-themed aesthetics with powerful focus-tracking features.

Whether you're studying for exams, working on projects, or developing new skills, Nebula Focus helps you stay focused, take smart breaks, and achieve your study goals.
